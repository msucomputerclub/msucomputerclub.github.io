MSU Computer Club

1 Normal Ave, Montclair, NJ 07043 - computerclub@montclair.edu

Computer Club is a Class IV SGA Organization. The purpose of Computer Club is to explore different areas of interest and create a social environment where students with various technology experiences can come together to share information with one another.

Contact

Feel free to send us an email at computerclub@montclair.edu or if you're a Red Hawk already you can visit our orgsync page for additional information @ orgsync.com

Club Activities

During club meetings we do a multitude of activities that vary meeting to meeting. Some of these activites include workshops, project showcases, data structures and algorithm problem sessions, and we even have alumni speakers who currently work in the industry.
